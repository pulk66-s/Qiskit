\title{Projection}
A Projection is a linear operator $P$ that can be applied from a vector $A$ to a vector $B$.
It represents the projection of $A$ on $B$.

\section{Definition}

\begin{equation}
    P_u(v) = \frac{v \cdot u}{u \cdot u}u
\end{equation}
